Description

This route runs Monday through Friday from 33rd & Market Streets at W.W. Hagerty Library to the Vidas Field House at 42nd and Powelton from 6 a.m. to 9 p.m. The shuttle also provides one express run from 33rd and Market to the Vidas Athletic Complex at 5:45 a.m., Monday through Friday. The shuttle does not run on weekends or holidays.
